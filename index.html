Political Space: Mapping Memetic Tribes

An interactive application that lets one explore the ideological positioning of memetic tribes across three axes.

Click to go forwards, right-click to go backwards. Hover over the tribe planets to see their information. Click on the planets to see further explanation. Planet are sized according to the amount of comments on their related subreddits.

Development is ongoing - suggestions can be emailed to sal@oilab.eu.
